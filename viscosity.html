Why Viscosity Matters

Viscosity is critical for selecting the right pump type and sizing. High-viscosity liquids require positive displacement pumps (gear, lobe, progressive cavity), while low-viscosity liquids work well with centrifugal pumps.

Pump Selection Guidelines

  • < 100 mPa·s: Centrifugal pumps work well
  • 100-1000 mPa·s: Consider gear or lobe pumps
  • > 1000 mPa·s: Progressive cavity or peristaltic pumps recommended
  • > 10000 mPa·s: Heated lines and specialized pumps needed

Material Quick Guide

HDPE →
High-Density Polyethylene
Best for: Acids, bases, alcohols. Common for drums & containers.
PP →
Polypropylene
Best for: Strong acids & bases. Higher temp resistance than PE.
PTFE →
Teflon®
Best for: Nearly all chemicals. Premium choice for seals & gaskets.
SS 316 →
Stainless Steel (1.4401)
Best for: Corrosive environments. Better than 304 for chlorides.
NBR (Nitrile) →
Buna-N Rubber
Best for: Oils, fuels, hydraulics. Most common seal material.
EPDM →
Ethylene Propylene
Best for: Water, steam, bases. NOT for oils/fuels.

About This Tool

ChemicalResistance.org helps engineers, safety professionals, and anyone handling chemicals find the right materials for safe storage and handling. Our database includes 990+ chemicals with resistance ratings for 12 common materials at two temperatures.

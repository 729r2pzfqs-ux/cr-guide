Why is HDPE resistant to Copper Vitriol Copper Sulfate?

HDPE shows excellent resistance to Copper Vitriol Copper Sulfate due to its tightly packed molecular structure and chemical inertness to most acids, bases, and salts.

⚠️ When NOT to use HDPE

Avoid using High-Density Polyethylene with strong oxidizing acids (nitric, chromic), chlorinated solvents, and aromatic hydrocarbons. Maximum service temperature is around 80°C.

Why is SS 316 resistant to Sulfuric Acid?

SS 316 shows good resistance to Sulfuric Acid due to its molybdenum content providing superior resistance to chlorides and corrosive chemicals compared to 304.

⚠️ When NOT to use SS 316

Avoid using Stainless Steel 316 with hydrochloric acid, hot sulfuric acid, and reducing environments. Best general-purpose stainless steel for chemical exposure.

FAQ

Is Stainless Steel 316 resistant to Sulfuric Acid?

Stainless Steel 316 has Good (B) resistance to Sulfuric Acid at 20°C.

Can I store Sulfuric Acid in Stainless Steel 316?

Yes, Stainless Steel 316 is rated B for Sulfuric Acid.

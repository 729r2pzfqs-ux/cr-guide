Why is Viton (FPM) resistant to Magnesium Chloride?

Viton (FPM) shows excellent resistance to Magnesium Chloride due to its fluorinated rubber structure providing exceptional resistance to oils, fuels, and many chemicals.

⚠️ When NOT to use Viton (FPM)

Avoid using Viton Fluoroelastomer with ketones, esters, amines, and hot water/steam. Excellent for fuel and oil applications.

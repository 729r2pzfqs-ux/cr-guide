FAQ

Is SAN resistant to Methanol?

SAN has Fair (C) resistance to Methanol at 20°C. Limited resistance - short-term exposure only.

Can I store Methanol in SAN containers?

Use caution - SAN is rated C (Fair) for Methanol.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Acetal (POM) resistant to Fotoentwickler?

Acetal (POM) has Excellent (A) resistance to Fotoentwickler at 20°C. Excellent resistance - recommended for continuous use.

Can I store Fotoentwickler in Acetal (POM) containers?

Based on the A rating at room temperature, Acetal (POM) is suitable for Fotoentwickler storage. At elevated temperatures (50°C), the rating changes to C. Always verify with your container supplier.

What affects chemical resistance?

Temperature, concentration, exposure duration, and mechanical stress all affect compatibility. Our ratings assume standard conditions - always test for critical applications.

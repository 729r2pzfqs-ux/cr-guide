Frequently Asked Questions

Can I store Dibromethan-1,2 in EPDM containers?

EPDM has a Poor (D) rating for Dibromethan-1,2 at room temperature (20°C). Not recommended - significant degradation expected. Always consider concentration, exposure time, and verify with your container supplier.

Does temperature affect EPDM's resistance to Dibromethan-1,2?

Yes. At elevated temperature (50°C), the rating changes to Poor (D). Higher temperatures typically reduce chemical resistance.

What materials work best for Dibromethan-1,2?

Why is Aluminium resistant to Potassium Fluoride?

Aluminium shows excellent resistance to Potassium Fluoride due to its protective oxide layer (Al₂O₃) that forms naturally and resists many chemicals.

⚠️ When NOT to use Aluminium

Avoid using Aluminium with strong acids (HCl, H₂SO₄), strong alkalis (NaOH), and mercury compounds which destroy the protective oxide layer.

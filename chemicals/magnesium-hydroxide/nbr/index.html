Why is NBR resistant to Magnesium Hydroxide?

NBR shows good resistance to Magnesium Hydroxide due to its acrylonitrile content providing resistance to oils, fuels, and many industrial chemicals.

⚠️ When NOT to use NBR

Avoid using Nitrile Rubber (Buna-N) with strong oxidizers, ketones, chlorinated solvents, and ozone. Not suitable for polar solvents.

FAQ

Is NBR resistant to Magnesium Hydroxide?

NBR has Good (B) resistance to Magnesium Hydroxide at 20°C. Good resistance - suitable for intermittent contact.

Can I store Magnesium Hydroxide in NBR containers?

Yes, NBR is rated B (Good) for Magnesium Hydroxide.

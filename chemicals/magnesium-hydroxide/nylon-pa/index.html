FAQ

Is Nylon (PA) resistant to Magnesium Hydroxide?

Nylon (PA) has Excellent (A) resistance to Magnesium Hydroxide at 20°C. Excellent resistance - recommended for continuous use.

Can I store Magnesium Hydroxide in Nylon (PA) containers?

Yes, Nylon (PA) is rated A (Excellent) for Magnesium Hydroxide.

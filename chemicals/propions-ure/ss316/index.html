Frequently Asked Questions

Can I store Propionsäure in Stainless Steel 316 containers?

Stainless Steel 316 has a Excellent (A) rating for Propionsäure at room temperature (20°C). Excellent resistance - suitable for continuous use. Always consider concentration, exposure time, and verify with your container supplier.

Does temperature affect Stainless Steel 316's resistance to Propionsäure?

Yes. At elevated temperature (50°C), the rating changes to Excellent (A). Higher temperatures typically reduce chemical resistance.

FAQ

Is Nylon (PA) resistant to Formaldehyde?

Nylon (PA) has Fair (C) resistance to Formaldehyde at 20°C. Limited resistance - short-term exposure only.

Can I store Formaldehyde in Nylon (PA) containers?

Use caution - Nylon (PA) is rated C (Fair) for Formaldehyde.

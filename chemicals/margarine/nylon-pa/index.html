Frequently Asked Questions

Can I store Margarine in Nylon (PA) containers?

Nylon (PA) has a Good (B) rating for Margarine at room temperature (20°C). Good resistance - suitable for limited exposure. Always consider concentration, exposure time, and verify with your container supplier.

Does temperature affect Nylon (PA)'s resistance to Margarine?

Yes. At elevated temperature (50°C), the rating changes to Good (B). Higher temperatures typically reduce chemical resistance.

FAQ

Is Polypropylene (PP) resistant to Potassium Chromate?

Polypropylene (PP) has Excellent (A) resistance to Potassium Chromate at 20°C. Excellent resistance - recommended for continuous use.

Can I store Potassium Chromate in Polypropylene (PP) containers?

Yes, Polypropylene (PP) is rated A (Excellent) for Potassium Chromate.

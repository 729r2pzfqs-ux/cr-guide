Frequently Asked Questions

Can I store Blei-(II)-acetat in HDPE containers?

HDPE has a Excellent (A) rating for Blei-(II)-acetat at room temperature (20°C). Excellent resistance - suitable for continuous use. Always consider concentration, exposure time, and verify with your container supplier.

Does temperature affect HDPE's resistance to Blei-(II)-acetat?

Yes. At elevated temperature (50°C), the rating changes to Excellent (A). Higher temperatures typically reduce chemical resistance.

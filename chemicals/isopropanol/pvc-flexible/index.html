Isopropanol Resistance of PVC Flexible

Is PVC Flexible compatible with Isopropanol? See the chemical resistance rating below.

Compatibility Rating

At 20°C (68°F)
D
Poor

At 50°C (122°F)
D
Poor

Why is PVC Flexible rated this way to Isopropanol?

PVC Flexible is not recommended for use with Isopropanol. Significant degradation or failure is expected.

⚠️ When NOT to use PVC Flexible

Avoid using PVC (Flexible/Plasticized) with the same solvents as rigid PVC, plus any chemical that extracts plasticizers. Generally less resistant than rigid PVC.

FAQ

Is PVC Flexible resistant to Isopropanol?

PVC Flexible has Poor (D) resistance to Isopropanol at 20°C. Not recommended - significant degradation expected.

Can I store Isopropanol in PVC Flexible containers?

Use caution - PVC Flexible is rated D (Poor) for Isopropanol.

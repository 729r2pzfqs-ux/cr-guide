Frequently Asked Questions

Is PVDF resistant to Isobutylacetat?

PVDF has Good (B) resistance to Isobutylacetat at 20°C. Good resistance - suitable for intermittent contact.

Can I store Isobutylacetat in PVDF containers?

Based on the B rating at room temperature, PVDF is suitable for Isobutylacetat storage. At elevated temperatures (50°C), the rating remains stable. Always verify with your container supplier.

What affects chemical resistance?

Temperature, concentration, exposure duration, and mechanical stress all affect compatibility. Our ratings assume standard conditions - always test for critical applications.

FAQ

Is Nylon (PA) resistant to Arsenic Acid?

Nylon (PA) has Fair (C) resistance to Arsenic Acid at 20°C. Limited resistance - short-term exposure only.

Can I store Arsenic Acid in Nylon (PA) containers?

Use caution - Nylon (PA) is rated C (Fair) for Arsenic Acid.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Polystyrene resistant to Potassium Cyanide?

Polystyrene has No Data (NR) resistance to Potassium Cyanide at 20°C. No data available.

Can I store Potassium Cyanide in Polystyrene containers?

Based on the NR rating at room temperature, Polystyrene may not be ideal for Potassium Cyanide storage. At elevated temperatures (50°C), the rating remains stable. Always verify with your container supplier.

What affects chemical resistance?

Temperature, concentration, exposure duration, and mechanical stress all affect compatibility. Our ratings assume standard conditions - always test for critical applications.

Why is Polycarbonate resistant to Sodium Sulfite?

Polycarbonate shows good resistance to Sodium Sulfite due to its carbonate group structure, though it has limited chemical resistance compared to other engineering plastics.

⚠️ When NOT to use Polycarbonate

Avoid using Polycarbonate with alkalis, amines, ketones, esters, and many organic solvents. Prone to stress cracking.

FAQ

Is Polycarbonate resistant to Sodium Sulfite?

Polycarbonate has Good (B) resistance to Sodium Sulfite at 20°C. Good resistance - suitable for intermittent contact.

Can I store Sodium Sulfite in Polycarbonate containers?

Yes, Polycarbonate is rated B (Good) for Sodium Sulfite.

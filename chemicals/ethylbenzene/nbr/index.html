Ethylbenzene Resistance of NBR

Is NBR compatible with Ethylbenzene? See the chemical resistance rating below.

At 50°C (122°F)

Why is NBR rated this way to Ethylbenzene?

NBR is not recommended for use with Ethylbenzene. Significant degradation or failure is expected.

⚠️ When NOT to use NBR

Avoid using Nitrile Rubber (Buna-N) with strong oxidizers, ketones, chlorinated solvents, and ozone. Not suitable for polar solvents.

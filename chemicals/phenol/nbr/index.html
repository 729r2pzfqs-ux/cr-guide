Frequently Asked Questions

Can I store Phenol in NBR containers?

NBR has a Poor (D) rating for Phenol at room temperature (20°C). Not recommended - significant degradation expected. Always consider concentration, exposure time, and verify with your container supplier.

Does temperature affect NBR's resistance to Phenol?

Yes. At elevated temperature (50°C), the rating changes to Poor (D). Higher temperatures typically reduce chemical resistance.

What materials work best for Phenol?

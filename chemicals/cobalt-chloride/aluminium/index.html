Cobalt Chloride Resistance of Aluminium

Is Aluminium compatible with Cobalt Chloride? See the chemical resistance rating below.

Compatibility Rating

At 20°C (68°F)
C
Fair
Limited resistance - short-term exposure only
At 50°C (122°F)
C
Fair
Limited resistance - short-term exposure only

Why is Aluminium rated this way to Cobalt Chloride?

Aluminium has limited resistance to Cobalt Chloride. Short-term exposure may be acceptable, but prolonged contact is not recommended.

⚠️ When NOT to use Aluminium

Avoid using Aluminium with strong acids (HCl, H₂SO₄), strong alkalis (NaOH), and mercury compounds which destroy the protective oxide layer.

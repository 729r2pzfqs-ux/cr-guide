Frequently Asked Questions

Can I store Fluorbenzol in PVC Flexible containers?

PVC Flexible has a No Data (NR) rating for Fluorbenzol at room temperature (20°C). No data available. Always consider concentration, exposure time, and verify with your container supplier.

Does temperature affect PVC Flexible's resistance to Fluorbenzol?

Yes. At elevated temperature (50°C), the rating changes to No Data (NR). Higher temperatures typically reduce chemical resistance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I store Antimonpentachlorid in Stainless Steel 304 containers?

Stainless Steel 304 has a Poor (D) rating for Antimonpentachlorid at room temperature (20°C). Not recommended - significant degradation expected. Always consider concentration, exposure time, and verify with your container supplier.

Does temperature affect Stainless Steel 304's resistance to Antimonpentachlorid?

Yes. At elevated temperature (50°C), the rating changes to Poor (D). Higher temperatures typically reduce chemical resistance.

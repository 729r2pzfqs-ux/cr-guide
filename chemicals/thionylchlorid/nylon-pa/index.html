Frequently Asked Questions

Can I store Thionylchlorid in Nylon (PA) containers?

Nylon (PA) has a Poor (D) rating for Thionylchlorid at room temperature (20°C). Not recommended - significant degradation expected. Always consider concentration, exposure time, and verify with your container supplier.

Does temperature affect Nylon (PA)'s resistance to Thionylchlorid?

Yes. At elevated temperature (50°C), the rating changes to Poor (D). Higher temperatures typically reduce chemical resistance.

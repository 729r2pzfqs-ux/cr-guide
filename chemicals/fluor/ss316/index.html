Frequently Asked Questions

Can I store Fluor in Stainless Steel 316 containers?

Stainless Steel 316 has a Poor (D) rating for Fluor at room temperature (20°C). Not recommended - significant degradation expected. Always consider concentration, exposure time, and verify with your container supplier.

Does temperature affect Stainless Steel 316's resistance to Fluor?

Yes. At elevated temperature (50°C), the rating changes to Poor (D). Higher temperatures typically reduce chemical resistance.

What materials work best for Fluor?

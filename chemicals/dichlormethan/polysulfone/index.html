Frequently Asked Questions

Can I store Dichlormethan in Polysulfone containers?

Polysulfone has a Poor (D) rating for Dichlormethan at room temperature (20°C). Not recommended - significant degradation expected. Always consider concentration, exposure time, and verify with your container supplier.

Does temperature affect Polysulfone's resistance to Dichlormethan?

Yes. At elevated temperature (50°C), the rating changes to Poor (D). Higher temperatures typically reduce chemical resistance.

What materials work best for Dichlormethan?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I store Chlorwasser in PVDF containers?

PVDF has a Excellent (A) rating for Chlorwasser at room temperature (20°C). Excellent resistance - suitable for continuous use. Always consider concentration, exposure time, and verify with your container supplier.

Does temperature affect PVDF's resistance to Chlorwasser?

Yes. At elevated temperature (50°C), the rating changes to Excellent (A). Higher temperatures typically reduce chemical resistance.

What materials work best for Chlorwasser?

Materials with excellent (A) resistance include: ECTFE/ETFE, FEP, PTFE, PVDF, Viton (FPM). See all materials →

FAQ

Is Nylon (PA) resistant to Calcium Oxide?

Nylon (PA) has Good (B) resistance to Calcium Oxide at 20°C. Good resistance - suitable for intermittent contact.

Can I store Calcium Oxide in Nylon (PA) containers?

Yes, Nylon (PA) is rated B (Good) for Calcium Oxide.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I store Kaliumchlorid in PTFE containers?

PTFE has a Excellent (A) rating for Kaliumchlorid at room temperature (20°C). Excellent resistance - suitable for continuous use. Always consider concentration, exposure time, and verify with your container supplier.

Does temperature affect PTFE's resistance to Kaliumchlorid?

Yes. At elevated temperature (50°C), the rating changes to Excellent (A). Higher temperatures typically reduce chemical resistance.

What materials work best for Kaliumchlorid?

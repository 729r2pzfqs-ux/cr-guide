Frequently Asked Questions

Can I store Schwefelsäure in EPDM containers?

EPDM has a Good (B) rating for Schwefelsäure at room temperature (20°C). Good resistance - suitable for limited exposure. Always consider concentration, exposure time, and verify with your container supplier.

Does temperature affect EPDM's resistance to Schwefelsäure?

Yes. At elevated temperature (50°C), the rating changes to No Data (NR). Higher temperatures typically reduce chemical resistance.

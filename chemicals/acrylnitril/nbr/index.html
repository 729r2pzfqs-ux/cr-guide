Frequently Asked Questions

Is NBR resistant to Acrylnitril?

NBR has Poor (D) resistance to Acrylnitril at 20°C. Not recommended - significant degradation expected.

Can I store Acrylnitril in NBR containers?

Based on the D rating at room temperature, NBR may not be ideal for Acrylnitril storage. At elevated temperatures (50°C), the rating remains stable. Always verify with your container supplier.

What affects chemical resistance?

Temperature, concentration, exposure duration, and mechanical stress all affect compatibility. Our ratings assume standard conditions - always test for critical applications.

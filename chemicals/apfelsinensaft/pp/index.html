Frequently Asked Questions

Is Polypropylene (PP) resistant to Apfelsinensaft?

Polypropylene (PP) has Excellent (A) resistance to Apfelsinensaft at 20°C. Excellent resistance - recommended for continuous use.

Can I store Apfelsinensaft in Polypropylene (PP) containers?

Based on the A rating at room temperature, Polypropylene (PP) is suitable for Apfelsinensaft storage. At elevated temperatures (50°C), the rating remains stable. Always verify with your container supplier.

What affects chemical resistance?

Temperature, concentration, exposure duration, and mechanical stress all affect compatibility. Our ratings assume standard conditions - always test for critical applications.
